The Fierce DPS

The DPS role is divided into three categories; Melee-DPS, Range-DPS and Magic-DPS. Here is an overall summary of the DPS role in general.

DPS translates to Damage Per Second, in other words, the attackers.
DPS have high offensive stats that contribute to doing damage, and therefore their duty is to attack and eliminate targets as fast as possible.
DPS make out the most of the party. It is normal to have two DPS in a four-man squad.

Their Limit Break is a special attack.

Melee-DPS

Melee-DPS attack with swords, knives, lances and so on. They have to get up close to the enemy to do serious damage, and have weaker ranged attacks.

With mediocre defense, the melee-DPS can take some damage before they fall to the ground.

Their Limit Break does high damage on ONE enemy.

Range-DPS

The range-DPS attacks from a distance with arrows, bullets or chakrams. Being able to attack over great ranges while moving makes them the most mobile category.

Range-DPS have an ability that increases movement speed outside of battle.

Their Limit Break does damge to all enemies in a straight line in front of them.

Magic-DPS

As the name says, they use magic, which means they often have to stand still and cast their spells, while also keeping an eye on how much magic they have remaining.

Magic-DPS have a big variety of spells, and can cast from far away. They don't have much defence and some attacks may interrupt their casting. Keep them from harm.

Their Limit Break will attack enemies in a targeted area.
