The Steady TANK

The TANK is always a melee job.

The TANK is the leader of the party, going ahead and keeping the enemies' attention. Making sure the other party members take as little damage as possible.
TANKS have lots of defence and deffensive abilities, keeping them standing while being attacked from all the enemies.

TANKS have some abilities that help with managing the enemies' attention, like taking it, giving it to others, and accumulating it.

Their Limit Break will give themselves and nearby allies a temporary defence boost.
