Tank Healer DPS

The Attentive HEALER

The HEALER is always a magic user.

The HEALER uses healing-magic to heal allies and remove status ailments. They also have some offensive spells to do damage, but their offensive stats are below average.
Healers do not have much defense, and can get knocked out easily, so they need to be kept from harm.

The healers each have an extra trait to their healing magic, depending on their job.
Some have the regen ability, which gradually restores health.
And some have the ability to put on a protective barrier on their targets, nullifying damage taken.

Their Limit Break heals allies in an area around them, and may ressurrect KO'd allies.
